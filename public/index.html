description

Generate a Resume

By creating a login, you can input your own information into the various resume fields. Once you select generate, res.create will populate a resume for you.

cloud

Save Your Data

A great plus to res.create is that once you create a login, any information that you add to your resume forms will be saved! No need to re-enter your resume information over and over again if you need to send your resume to a future employer..

Resume Templates

Choose from a variety of templates to display your resume information with.
